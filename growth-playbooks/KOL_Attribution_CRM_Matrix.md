# KOL Pipeline & On-Chain Attribution CRM Framework
*An Enterprise System Design for High-Conversion Partner Sourcing and Capital Allocation*

This framework outlines the operational infrastructure used to source, evaluate, and track Key Opinion Leaders (KOLs) and affiliate networks. It shifts marketing execution away from vanity impressions (views, likes) and anchors partner incentives directly to on-chain liquidity injection, trading volume, and wallet retention loops.

## 2. On-Chain Attribution & Conversion Funnel Logic

To prevent capital wastage, every partner is mapped to a unique referral parameter embedded into the protocol front-end or smart contract interface.

### Churn Mitigation and Retention Triggers:
* **The Trailing 14-Day Evaluation Rule:** If an activated partner’s on-chain conversion rate (Wallets Connected vs. Wallets Transacting) drops below **1.5%** over a trailing 14-day window, their campaign profile automatically flags for structural adjustment.
* **The Structural Pivot:** Rather than canceling the partnership completely, the account pivots from a fixed retainer model to a **pure volume-share or performance-based CPA structure**. This protects the protocol's capital runway, filters out fake social media engagement, and rewards partners driving authentic liquidity.
